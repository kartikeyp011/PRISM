Confined Space Entry Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Document ID: sop-confined-space-001

1. Scope
This SOP governs entry into permit-required confined spaces including tanks, vessels, and enclosed process areas.

2. Atmospheric requirements
- Minimum acceptable oxygen level: 19.5%
- Maximum oxygen level: 23.5%
- Continuous atmospheric monitoring is mandatory during occupancy
- Entry supervisor must verify readings before each shift entry

3. Permit requirements
- Confined space entry permit must be active before any worker enters
- At least one attendant must remain outside the confined space at all times
- Rescue equipment must be staged at the entry point before work begins

4. Worker restrictions
No worker may enter a confined space when:
- Oxygen is below 19.5% or above 23.5%
- Toxic gas levels exceed permissible exposure limits
- The entry permit has expired or been cancelled

5. Stop work authority
Any worker or attendant may stop work if atmospheric conditions deteriorate or permit conditions are violated.
